Our Story

Rooted on the Pacific coast—slow travel, small groups, and nature first.

Why We Built Pacific Trails

Pacific Trails Resort began with a simple idea: nature first, itinerary second. Our coastal property sits along miles of maritime chaparral and intertidal trails where guests slow down, breathe the ocean air, and reconnect with what matters. Over the years we’ve curated small-group experiences—guided hikes, serene kayak routes at sunrise, bird-watching walks led by local naturalists, and evening yoga sessions in our ocean-view pavilion. Our team focuses on low-impact travel, partnering with local stewards to protect native species and restore trails. Whether you’re here to reset, celebrate, or simply wander, we’ll help you craft a retreat that feels personal, grounded, and unforgettable.

Our Values

  • Stewardship: Trails maintained with leave-no-trace practices.
  • Wellbeing: Daily yoga, quiet hours, and tech-light zones.
  • Local: Partnerships with guides, artisans, and marine educators.
  • Access: Clear maps, beginner-friendly routes, and inclusive pricing.
