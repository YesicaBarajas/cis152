Enjoy Nature Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort is a place I would describe as calm, simple, and relaxing. The idea is to give people a chance to enjoy nature without stress. Here you can wake up and hear the ocean, take a walk on the trails, or just sit outside and breathe fresh air. During the day there are hikes, kayaking, and bird watching, but you can also take time for yourself and rest. At night, guests enjoy dinner with a view and sleep in a private yurt that feels cozy and different from a regular hotel. What I like about Pacific Trails is how it mixes comfort with outdoor life. It is the kind of place where you make good memories and feel happy to come back again.

  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours
  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying complimentary appetizers and beverages
  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view
