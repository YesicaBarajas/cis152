What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts finished?

Each yurt includes a queen-size bed with a down quilt and a gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and dress in light layers for changing weather. It’s also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
