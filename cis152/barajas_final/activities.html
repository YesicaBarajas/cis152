Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use. Guests can enjoy a calm morning paddle or an adventurous ride along the coast.

Bird Watching

Bird watching is great any time at Pacific Trails. We also offer guided sunrise trips with local experts several times a week.
